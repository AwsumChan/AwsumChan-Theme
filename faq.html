Global Rules

These rules apply to boards of all categories.

  1. You must be legal age to view adult material in your country, state, or province of residence.
  2. Do not spam or flood the boards
  3. Do not advertise your website or imageboard. If you are looking to form a partnership with AwsumChan, please contact Circlepuller in our IRC channel for any inquiries. We'll make exceptions when appropriate.
  4. Do not post, request, or link to any illegal content. This includes child pornography, revenge porn, or pirated copyright content. Violation will result in a permanent ban from all boards.
  5. Content that doesn't fit in any particular board, belongs in /ra/.
  6. Keep all non-worksafe content in the NSFW categorized boards.
  7. As with 4chan, 7chan, or any other imageboard — please use common sense!
